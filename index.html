Hi! My name is Harry and welcome to my website.

Here you can find some of my work as well as my story as I become a professional in the creative field.

I’m currently a first year student at the University of Portsmouth studying Film Production BA Hons.

I began studying film when I was in secondary school, I was lucky enough to have a school which supported that GCSE and some very passionate teachers to teach me about it.

I received an A for my practical work which got me recognised by a VFX house on Carnaby Street.

I also studied drama which of course goes hand in hand.

I then studied a BTEC in creative media as I am much more of a ‘hands on’ type of person when it comes to learning which gave me wonderful access to better equipment and training; which gave me a diverse skill set.

Now I study a BA in Film Production at the University of Portsmouth, a prestigious and well-known university which I hope will teach me all I need to know so that I can finally break into the industry.
